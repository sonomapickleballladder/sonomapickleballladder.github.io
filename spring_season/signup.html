The initial registration deadline for the Open Ladder of this Spring Season is April 25th 2023. If you are signing up for any of the other separate Ladders I am offering dependent on how many sign-ups they get, the registration deadline is April 20th 2023. (Completing your registration for any Ladder by its respective registration deadline makes you eligible to play from the first round).

Note: You may still sign-up and join at any time after the initial deadline or after the Ladder is under way (no discounted fee).

Register:

    Step 1. Fill out and submit the Sign-up Form

    Step 2. Make your Payment

    Note: Your registration will not be considered complete until you have completed both of these steps. (If you are signing up for any of the separate Ladders that are being offered, you can wait to pay until you hear whether or not it is going ahead).

Administrative Fees:

    Total your amount due as follows for each Ladder you sign up for:

    Administrative Registration Fee: $10
    (covers administrative time: getting you into the system, establishing your initial rating and placement, communications, answering questions)

    PLUS

    Administrative Seasonal Fee: $30
    (covers administrative time: entering scores, determining groups, communications, keeping website and spreadsheets updated, troubleshooting)

    TOTAL: $40

    Note: These fees have been calculated based on the anticipated initial and weekly time commitment running the Ladder will require of me (the Administrator). Hopefully, they are not a barrier to entry. If they are, please email me and I may be able to provide a discount.

    *** If you wish to add an optional donation amount in recognition of the time I spent doing all the preparatory development work necessary to set up and offer this Ladder, that would be very welcome! ***
