 Where are matches played?

 Do I have to be a certain skill level to join?
All skill levels are very welcome! Previous seasons have had players ranging from around 2.5 to 5.0+. Anyone wishing to participate however, should at least have a solid understanding of the rules of the game, and ideally some prior experience playing competitively. If you're concerned there won't be enough other players around your exact skill level, try to encourage as many similarly-skilled players as possible to also sign up so as to ensure your ladder experience is an enjoyable and competitive one.
 Will it work for me to join the Ladder if I will be away certain weeks?
Yes, the Ladder is set up to accommodate players traveling! You can skip any round if you're not available and pick up where you left off the next round.

 I signed up but didn't receive a confirmation email.
 I am unable to log in.
Firstly, make sure you have signed up AND confirmed your email address. Then check that you are entering your email (not your name) and secret key exactly as you did on the Sign-up Form. Please also make sure you are not trying to use any credentials / saved logins you might have from previous ladder seasons, as these are only valid on a season by season basis.

If you think you have entered everything correctly but it still isn't working, please email me. I will send you a one-time magic link, which should get you into your dashboard. From there, you can copy your permanent login link under where it says 'Messages' and make note of the credentials it autofills when you open it.
 I forgot my secret key.
Check if you can view your responses to the Signup Form and unhide your secret key (you may not be able to do so depending on your browser settings, or if you're using a different device/browser than you used to register).

If not, you can send me an email and I will send you a link which you can use to log in. If you email me before the initial registration deadline, you may not get a response until after the deadline, as you shouldn't need to log in before then.
 Can I pay with Venmo?
Yes. Please refer to the payment section on the signup page.
 Can I join any additional Ladders to the one(s) I initially signed up for later?
Yes, but it's simpler if you just sign up for all the ones you wish to be in initially.
 Can I switch Ladders later (after signing up initially)?
You can up until the initial registration deadline by simply emailing me.
 How can I change information I entered incorrectly in the Signup Form?
You can email me the desired changes and I will actualize them.
 Why is the round length 2 weeks this season?
I have received feedback that 1-week rounds feel too short when I have tried them in the past, and also that 2-week rounds feel very spread out. I'm hoping that 2 weeks can make for a better balance.
 Why are the other ladder formats that have been offered in the past (skinny singles, singles, men's only, etc..) not being offered this season?
Because these have not generated very much interest previously, and there is a lot of setup work involved in offering more ladders, which I do not wish to undertake unless I am reasonably certain there is a significant amount of interest. If you know of a lot of people who would like to participate in a ladder format which is not currently being offered, you can let me know by email and I will certainly consider offering it again in the future.
 How is my information secured/protected?
All the information you submit through the Sign-up Form is stored in an encrypted database, which only I as the administrator can access. None of it will ever be shared with or sold to any 3rd party entities. Certain information though, such as your name, email, and phone number if you choose to provide one, will be entered into a spreadsheet which is visible to the other players in whichever ladder(s) you are in.

When signing up, you are welcome to use an email alias if you don't want your real email address to be visible. You don't have to provide your real name either if you prefer not to. Please do not provide any information which would cause you alarm if someone else were to somehow get their hands on it. Your secret key should not contain any confidential information, or be the same as a password you use for any important account. If you are hesitant to provide any of the information asked for, please let me know. However, most of it is necessary to run the service.
 A certain page isn't loading.
Check your internet connection and open it on another device or in a different browser if possible. If this doesn't make a difference, please email me with as much info as you can provide and I will do my best to troubleshoot.
