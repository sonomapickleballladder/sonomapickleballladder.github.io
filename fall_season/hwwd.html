🪜 How it Works: What's Different 🪜

This is how I propose the Singles & Mini-Singles Ladders will work differently than the other doubles Ladders. I may further change certain aspects of the format if I think of better ideas, and I am also open to suggestions. For now though, this seems to me like the most reasonable, worthwhile and interesting approach.

Groupings:

  • Instead of groups of 4, players will be grouped into pools of 3 every round.

Format:

  • The format is a 3 player round-robin. Each player will face off against the other 2 players in their pool, playing the same number of games (2 games to 15 by default) against each.

Scheduling:

  • If all 3 players in a pool are not able to meet on the same day, that is not a problem. Head to head matches against each player in your pool can be played on separate days.
